According to websites like statefarm.com, cars.com and gecio.com, you should do the followwing when you are in a traffic accident.

Keep calm.

Relocate to safe location but do not leave the scene of the crime.

Check if anyone is hurt.

Contact the police/ Call 911.

Exchange driver informotion.

File a claim.

Make sure your car/bike is ready to go back onto the road.
